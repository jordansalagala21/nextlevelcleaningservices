STEP 1: Create a Formspree Account
-----------------------------------
1. Go to: https://formspree.io
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email address (the one where you want to receive quote requests)
4. Create a password
5. Verify your email

STEP 2: Create Your Form
-------------------------
1. After logging in, click "New Form" or "+ New Project"
2. Give it a name like "Next Level Cleaning Quotes"
3. Formspree will give you a unique Form ID (looks like: xyzabc12)
4. Copy this Form ID

STEP 3: Update Your Website
----------------------------
1. Open index.html
2. Find this line (around line 2072):
   <form action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST">
   
3. Replace YOUR_FORM_ID with your actual Form ID from Step 2
   Example: <form action="https://formspree.io/f/xyzabc12" method="POST">
   
4. Save the file
